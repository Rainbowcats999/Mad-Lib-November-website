Now this kid is a true legend, where he came from and where he got to today is truly an amazing and inspirational story. Daniel LaRusso, 18, who moved from Newark, New Jersey to Recita, Califronia. He never wanted this kind of life, moving away from all his friends and only being with his single, struggling mom. He deeply hated his new living environment, a small, filthy old apartment was not the kind of living condition that he was used to, but sadly for Daniel, this was just all his family could afford. On his first day of school, Daniel meets a girl and instantly falls in love with her, her name was Ally. Just as he thought that this could make his misery better, boy was he wrong. Little did he know, that his new crush, was actully the ex-girlfriend of his new soon to be bully, Johhny Lawernce. When Daniel was offically a student at that new school, Johnny became his rival, which made Daniel hate that environment even more.
